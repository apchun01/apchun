Alexander P. Chun

Hi! I'm Alex. I'm currently pusuing a PhD in American Studies at Brown University.

My research and teaching interests are in the nexus of media studies, queer studies, and Asian/American studies.

I'm currently working on my dissertation project, tentatively titled "Sex in the Digital Age."

Perhaps deceptively simple, the question at the heart of my dissertation, is as follows: If, as Foucault asserts, the production of sex and sexualities are historically contingent, how are sex and sexualities being produced and negotiated in our present moment—the digital age? My approaches to answering this question, however, are less straight-forward, as my sites of study and objects of analysis are rife with contradictions, illegibilities, struggles, and ethical opacities.
